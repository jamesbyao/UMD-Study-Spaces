About

Finding a quiet space on campus to study can be difficult. It can be extrememly difficult if you are a new student on campus. We hope to provide a quick and easy way to find a quiet space on campus for you whether you are a freshmen or senior. Just fill the form below and a study spot will appear based on your preferences. If you are unsure what locations you are looking for, check out the locations tab! If you have any questions, feel free to reach out to us!

Factors to Consider

When looking for a study spot, there are numerous factors to consider. Please have a few ideas in mind when filling out the form below. The factors used to determine a spot are as follows: campus quadrant, noise level, number of tables, and time of day.
